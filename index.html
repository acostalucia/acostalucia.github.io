Donate Now

With your donation you help the following causes:

  • Education

    Your donation ensures a formal, safe and dignified education for many children and adolescents in rural schools.

  • Strengthening communities

    Your donation gives our partner communities all the tools they need to become stronger.

  • Women empowerment

    Your donation ensures the training, empowerment and protection of many girls and women.

  • Environmental protection

    Your donation allows us to develop environmental protection projects and protect those affected by climate change.

Your bequest—large or small—makes a big difference to Voices on the Border!

Remembering Voices on the Border in your estate plans is one of the most important steps you can take to help secure a better future for the work we are doing today-and tomorrow. Your generosity will live on in projects that help improve lives, communities and the world as a whole. Your legacy will be an inspiration to others and reflect your values and commitment to the causes you deeply believe in.

You can include Voices in your will or living trust. Another popular way to provide for Voices is to simply designate Voices a beneficiary of your IRA or qualified retirement plan with Fidelity, Putnam, Vanguard, etc. You can contact us by Email or call us to help you with this process.
